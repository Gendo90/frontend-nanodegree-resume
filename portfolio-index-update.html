Patrick Gendotti

Patrick Gendotti is a software developer with an M.S. from the University of Southern California and currently lives in the San Francisco Bay Area. He has worked professionally for two companies, an early-stage startup and a government subcontractor, delivering working MVPs at each. Both MVPs consisted of microservices, and were built with extensive scalability in mind. He has experience across the stack with React, Angular, Spring Boot, Node.js, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL, as well as with Docker microservices, Jenkins CI/CD pipelines, and cloud deployment to providers such as AWS, GCP, and OCP.
